SmoovCoin Tokenomics

SmoovCoin is built around a simple idea: fixed supply, clear early liquidity, and real music-driven demand. No confusing math, no runaway emissions.

100% Transparent Launch

Total Supply

10,000,000 SMV

Permanently capped, no inflation.

Creator Buy-In

50% at Genesis

JP Cali Smoov purchases 5,000,000 SMV to seed liquidity and show skin in the game.

Public & Ecosystem

50% for the Community

Liquidity, incentives, grants, and a community treasury.

1. Token Creator Allocation — 50%

At launch, the creator purchases 5,000,000 SMV (50%) of the supply. This:

  • Establishes early liquidity and a natural price floor
  • Shows founder confidence and real economic commitment
  • Helps stabilize the market in the earliest days of trading

Creator-owned tokens may later be subject to vesting or lock-up terms if a governance or vesting module is introduced.

2. Public & Ecosystem Allocation — 50%

Public Liquidity & Market Launch 20% • 2,000,000 SMV

AMM pools, DEX listings, and liquidity for trading.

Ecosystem Incentives 15% • 1,500,000 SMV

Rewards for early adopters, playlists, and engagement quests.

Artist & Partner Grants 10% • 1,000,000 SMV

Incentivizing musicians, collabs, and exclusive GALA Music releases.

Community Treasury 5% • 500,000 SMV

Governance, platform experiments, and future marketing.

Fixed Supply

The 10M SMV cap is permanent. As demand grows, scarcity becomes part of the value story.

Demand Drivers

  • Track and album purchases
  • Artist-led campaigns and drops
  • Engagement rewards and playlists
  • Secondary market trading & collectibles

Liquidity Strategy

A portion of supply is dedicated to DEX/AMM liquidity pools, paired with stablecoins or native chain assets to support low-slippage trading and healthy price discovery.

Utility: What SmoovCoin Is For

SmoovCoin is built to plug into GALA Music first, with room to expand into the broader GALA ecosystem over time.

Track & Collectible Purchases

SmoovCoin aims to become a preferred payment method for:

  • Individual tracks and singles
  • Album collectibles and bundles
  • Exclusive or limited releases
  • Artist-created digital goods

Creator & Fan Engagement

Artists can experiment with new Smoov-powered experiences:

  • Rewarding superfans and top supporters
  • Unlocking gated content and secret drops
  • Revenue-sharing experiments and collabs

Future Platform Utility

Possible future extensions of SmoovCoin include:

  • Staking for platform perks or boost
  • Access passes for exclusive drops
  • Community governance participation
  • Cross-platform spend across GALA games & film

Governance (Planned / Optional)

A future governance module may allow SmoovCoin holders to help steer ecosystem decisions — such as how rewards are allocated, which artist grants get prioritized, and how the community treasury is deployed.

  • Ecosystem reward distribution
  • Artist & partner grant programs
  • Treasury spending and experiments
  • Utility upgrades and roadmap proposals

Governance features are not guaranteed and may be introduced later depending on adoption, regulation, and community interest.

Roadmap

The roadmap is intentionally simple and focused: ship the token, wire it into real music utilities, then expand where it makes sense.

Phase 1

Token Launch

  • Deploy SmoovCoin smart contract
  • Creator purchase of 50% supply
  • Seed initial liquidity pools
  • Begin integrating track purchases via GALA Music
Phase 2

Platform Integration

  • Onboard additional artists and partners
  • Exclusive track drops purchasable in SmoovCoin
  • Engagement rewards for holders and playlist curators
Phase 3

Expansion

  • Staking and governance modules
  • Deeper cross-GALA ecosystem utility (games, film, more)
  • Broader partner integrations & collabs
