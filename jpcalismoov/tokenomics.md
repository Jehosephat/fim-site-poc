SmoovCoin Tokenomics Whitepaper
1. Introduction
SmoovCoin is a digital asset designed to power a new era of music ownership, discovery, and creator–fan alignment. Built for seamless integration with the GALA Music ecosystem, SmoovCoin enables users to directly purchase tracks, support artists, and participate in an increasingly decentralized media landscape.
With a fixed total supply and a clear early liquidity model, SmoovCoin's tokenomics are designed to establish stability while maintaining long-term growth potential driven by platform utility and market adoption.

Establishing liquidity and a price floor
